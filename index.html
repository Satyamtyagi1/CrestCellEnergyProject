Solar FAQs

Does the government provide a solar subsidy in Uttar Pradesh?

Yes. Residential rooftop systems are eligible for central subsidy under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, disbursed after your system is installed and net-metered. Crest Solar Energies prepares and files this application for you.

Is solar a good option?

Yes — Most homes across India receive ample sunlight throughout the year, making rooftop solar a smart investment. Since households use a significant amount of electricity during the day, solar can reduce monthly power bills, provide long-term savings, and lower dependence on grid electricity.

Can I get net metering in Ghaziabad?

Yes, through PVVNL (Paschimanchal Vidyut Vitran Nigam), the local distribution company. We handle the application, inspection scheduling and meter-swap coordination as part of every install.

How long does installation take?

A typical residential rooftop system is installed within a few days once materials are on site; net-metering approval timelines depend on PVVNL's process and are outside our direct control.
